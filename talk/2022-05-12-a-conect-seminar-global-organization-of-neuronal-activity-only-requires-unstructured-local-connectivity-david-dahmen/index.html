2022-05-12 : A CONECT seminar "Global organization of neuronal activity only requires unstructured local connectivity" (David Dahmen)

CONECT seminar by David Dahmen.

Laurent U Perrinet

Date
2022-05-12 2:30 PM

During this CONECT seminar, David Dahmen will present his recent work on “Global organization of neuronal activity only requires unstructured local connectivity”:

Dahmen D, Layer M, Deutz L, Dąbrowska PA, Voges N, von Papen M, Brochier T, Riehle A, Diesmann M, Grün S, Helias M. Elife. 2022 Jan 20;11:e68422

Dr. David Dahmen is a PostDoc at the Research Centre Jülich (Institute of Neuroscience and Medicine (INM-6), Computational and Systems Neuroscience & Institute for Advanced Simulation (IAS-6), Theoretical Neuroscience & JARA-Institut Brain structure-function relationships (INM-10)).
